03 Meet your inspector

Brett Stephens

Built on four decades
of real experience.

Licensed BuilderHIA RegisteredCertified Inspector

With more than four decades working in building and construction, Brett has developed extensive practical knowledge across residential construction.

As a Licensed Builder, HIA member and Certified Building Inspector, Brett understands how homes are built—from foundations to roofing—and knows what to look for when assessing a property’s condition.

His broad hands-on experience means every inspection is thorough, professional and focused on identifying defects, safety concerns and maintenance issues that could affect your investment.

Whether you’re purchasing your first home, adding to your investment portfolio or preparing a property for sale, you can rely on Six Star Property Inspections for clear, accurate and independent advice.
